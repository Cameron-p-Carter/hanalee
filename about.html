About

Biography

A Life in Music

I'm a dedicated and passionate violinist with a deep love for sharing the power of music. Whether I'm performing on stage or teaching in the studio, I'm committed to delivering heartfelt, meaningful musical experiences tailored to each audience and occasion.


My performances span solo recitals, chamber music, and orchestral settings. I've performed as a soloist with the Bluescope Youth Orchestra touring New Zealand, led the KUSCI Piano Quartet, and performed Mozart's Violin Concerto No. 5 as a soloist at the Wollongong Town Hall and Bowral Arts Centre.
