About

Biography

A Life in Music

Hana Lee is a passionate and dedicated violinist whose artistry captivates audiences with its warmth and sincerity. With years of rigorous training and performance experience, she has developed a distinctive voice on the instrument that blends technical mastery with deep musical expression.


Her performances span solo recitals, chamber music collaborations, and orchestral settings, bringing classical repertoire to life for diverse audiences.

Education

Training & Credentials

Hana holds a degree in music performance, having studied under distinguished mentors who shaped her approach to both performance and pedagogy. Her education at the Wollongong Conservatorium of Music provided a strong foundation in classical tradition while encouraging artistic exploration and growth.


She continues to refine her craft through masterclasses, festival participation, and collaborative projects with fellow musicians.
